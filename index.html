Our Story

Born in a kitchen.
Built for a generation
that reads labels.

In 2022, our founder Priya Menon walked the snack aisle of a Bengaluru supermarket and read every label. Palm oil. Artificial colour 102. Maltodextrin. Flavour enhancer 621. She walked out empty-handed and went home to make her own.

Zestora started from that kitchen. Today we work directly with 12 farm partners across Karnataka, Rajasthan, and Gujarat — sourcing ingredients you can actually spell.
